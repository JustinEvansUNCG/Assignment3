Speedy Bikes

Speedy bikes is a bicycle company that operates in Greensboro NC, and has the objective of helping cyclists have the fastest bicycles in town. Speedy Bikes also hosts a quarterly cycle street race where cyclists we have a route to ride and try to get the the finish.
