Quarterly Speedy Cycles Street Race

The quarterly Speedy Cycles Street Race is an event held 4 times a year
to get people of all demographics and ages together to all participate
in something they will never forget. The race last several hours and has
prizes for the top contestants in each of the age categories. The profits
from admissions to the race all go to a variety of charities helping those in need.
